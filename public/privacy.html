Last updated: April 20, 2026

WoofWell ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Mind Proof Press. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app and website at woofwell.vercel.app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use WoofWell:

  • Account information: Email address and password when you sign up.
  • Usage data: Features you use, scans performed, and interaction data.
