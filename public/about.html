About WoofWell

AI-powered dog health tools for every pet owner

WoofWell is a free web application that gives dog owners access to AI-powered health tools that were previously only available through expensive veterinary consultations or scattered across unreliable sources online.

We believe every dog owner deserves fast, accurate, and easy-to-understand health information. Whether you're a first-time puppy parent or a lifelong dog lover with a multi-dog household, WoofWell is built to help you make better health decisions for the dogs in your care.

What WoofWell Offers

  • AI Breed Identifier — Upload a photo and instantly identify any dog's breed with detailed breed information.
  • Symptom Checker — Describe symptoms and get AI-powered triage guidance to help you decide when to see a vet.
  • 24/7 AI Vet Chat — Get answers to dog health questions any time of day, personalized to your dog's breed and age.
  • Health Profiles — Comprehensive breed-specific health reports covering common conditions, lifespan, exercise, diet, and grooming.
  • Vaccination Records — Track immunizations and get reminders when boosters are due.
  • Weight Tracker — Log and chart your dog's weight over time to monitor healthy trends.
  • Health Journal — Record daily observations and build a health history to share with your vet.
  • Vet Reminders — Never miss a checkup, dental cleaning, or flea treatment again.
  • Breed Comparison — Compare two breeds side by side across health, exercise, grooming, and more.
  • Dog Tools — Dog age converter and feeding calculator for everyday decisions.
  • Emergency Guide — Step-by-step first aid guides for common dog emergencies.
  • Multi-Dog Support — Manage unlimited dogs with separate health records for each.

Our Mission

Dogs give us unconditional love and loyalty. Our mission is to help dog owners give back by making it easier to understand, track, and protect their dog's health. WoofWell is not a substitute for professional veterinary care — but it is a powerful tool to help you be a more informed and proactive dog owner.

Technology

WoofWell is powered by Claude, Anthropic's state-of-the-art AI model, which is used for breed identification, symptom analysis, health profile generation, and the AI vet chat feature. User data is securely stored using Supabase. The application is hosted on Vercel.

Who We Are

WoofWell is built and maintained by Mind Proof Press, an independent software development studio focused on building useful, AI-powered tools for everyday people.
